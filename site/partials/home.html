Kiwi TV

Your source for watching all of your favorite live Internet Television (IPTV) channels.

- Watch and favorite channels from around the world. - Access 20+ built-in default channels. - Add custom channels from almost any internet stream. - Search and add channels from Twitch.tv. - Cast channels to connected devices such as Xbox One. - Sync your channel list and favorites across your Windows devices.

I hope you enjoy this app, and look forward to hearing any feedback you may have to make it even better!
