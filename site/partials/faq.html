How do I restore a default channel I removed?

In order to restore channels, you must go to Settings within the application, and select "Restore Default Channels" this will re-add any channels you deleted, and will unfavorite any default channels you have favorited.

How do I remove a channel I no longer want?

You can do this by either right clicking (on PC), or long-pressing on the channel in the channel list. When you do this, a menu will appear allowing you to remove the channel.
